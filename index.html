Process

How Keheilan works

01

Operators list farms

Farm operators create listings with their crop details, size, target raise, and expected returns. The AI Risk Scorer runs automatically on submission.

02

Admin approves listings

Platform administrators review AI risk scores and farm details, then approve or flag listings before they appear to investors.

03

Investors commit capital

Investors browse AI-matched deals, review yield forecasts and composite risk scores, then invest directly into approved farm listings.
